About Me

Ever since I was little, I've had an insatiable drive for creativity and learning. In fact for most of my life, one of my primary passions was music. Starting with guitar and gradually increasing to more and more instruments I grew up absolutely enamored the amount of creativity and problem solving an instrument can bring. Then soon after I graduated high school I found a second creative outlet for myself. One I enjoyed even more than music, and that was computer programing.

After a few years, hundreds of hours of programming, and most of a computer science degree under my belt, I finally decided to attend Flatiron School where I can really cultivate my passion for code. Here I learned truly, how much more I have to learn, and the huge amounts of excitement that brings me just like learning that new instrument for the first time.

So now, no matter where I end up. As long as there are problems to solve or new things to learn, I am positive my passion will never end.
